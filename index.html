How to make Creme Brulee - by Ashley Brown

Creme Brulee is a classic french desert made from cream and eggs and a touch of vanilla with a delicious burnt caramel topping.
Impress your friends with this mouth-watering delight!

Ingredients

  • 450ml Double Cream
  • 1 vanilla pod or a few drops of vanilla essence
  • 6 egg yolks
  • 70g castor sugar
  • Demarara sugar to caramelize

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 150 degrees
  2. Put cream into a saucepan. Split the vanilla pod in half and scrape out the seeds.
    Add the seeds to the cream or alternatively add the vanilla essence to the cream.
    Bring up to boil and remove from heat, and allow to cool for a little.
  3. Beat egg yolk and sugar together in a bowl.Pour in the cream and whisk to combine.
  4. Pass the liquid through a sieve and divide the mixture evenly between 5 ramekins.
  5. Put the ramekins in a deep sided oven tray and pour in enough hot water to come half way up the side of the ramekin.
  6. Bake in the centre of the oven for 40 to 50 minutes. The mixture should be a little wobbly when cooked.
  7. Leave to cool overnight in your fridge, then sprinkle with demerara sugar and place under a very hot grill to caramelise the top.
  8. Enjoy your delicious desert with friends and family!
